Get Out More

Get Out More is a PSA created to promote a healthier lifestyle as a result of the enclosed lifestyle we experience in the pandemic. Tasked as an assignment for a Film Production class at the University of British Columbia, the PSA is to convey a clear and concise message within the given timeframe of a minute and thirty seconds.

He's X,Y, and Z

Music played an integral part of growing up, and I wanted to create a work to honor the artists praised in my early adolescent years. Combining the practice of collage from my art history and visual arts courses with my digital medium preference, I presented a multifaceted work exploring the dynamic lives of individuals I tried to relate to, in order to understand my own.

While also striving to make curate meaningful user experiences through empathy and understanding.
